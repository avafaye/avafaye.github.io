Our Story

Welcome to Ava's Food Kitchen: a restaurant inspired by the philosophy that food should make you feel better, not worse. In 2018 we opened our first location in San Luis Obispo with a big idea in mind. We wanted to create healthy, farm to table dishes, using the best ingredients that California has to offer. We have partnered with local farms and suppliers to create our menu, and depending on the season our menu will change and reflect what is available.

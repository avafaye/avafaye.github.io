Introduction

The Roasted Bean

The Roasted Bean is San Luis Obispo's most sought after specialty coffee shop. Located in the heart of downtown, we are committed to making people's lives better through great coffee. We have an amazing team of baristas and staff members who love being able to serve our community every day.

Target audience

Being a specialty coffee shop, our target audience focuses on customers who want a coffee shop experience rather than a grab-and go. However we believe coffee should be accessbile to anyone, so we have worked with our partners to create a price point reachable to anybody and everybody. If anyone is on the look out for a delicious cup of coffe, The Roasting Bean is the answer. In addition, our customers are able to shop on our website for bags of beans. The people using our site vary from customers who went to our store location, and individuals looking to purchase coffee beans wholesale online.

This site is mainly used for users who want to learn about our coffee process, location in San Luis Obispo, as well view menu items and shop online. Our site will inlcude the types of coffee we roast (flavor notes, orignis and specific ingredients) as well as information about our roastery. When shopping, customers are able to view types of beans we carry with information about how each type of bean is different. This will edcate the user to make an informed decision about what type of beans they chose to purchase. In addtion to these tasks, the goals of our website users consist of locating our store location, understanding our company brand, and buy coffee online.
